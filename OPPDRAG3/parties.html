Political Party Information

Høyre

Høyre, short for Høyre (Høyre, literally "Right"), is a center-right political party in Norway. It advocates for free-market capitalism, individual freedoms, and a limited government role in the economy.

Arbeiderpartiet

Arbeiderpartiet, often referred to as the Labour Party, is a center-left political party in Norway. It focuses on social democracy, workers' rights, and a mixed-market economy with a strong welfare state.

Senterpartiet

Senterpartiet, known as the Centre Party, is a centrist political party in Norway. It emphasizes rural and agricultural issues, local governance, and a balanced regional development policy.

MDG (Miljøpartiet De Grønne)

MDG, the Green Party (Miljøpartiet De Grønne), focuses on environmental issues, sustainability, and social justice. It advocates for a green transition, renewable energy, and climate-conscious policies.

FRP Fremskrittspartiet

FrP, the Progress Party (Fremskrittspartiet), is a right-wing political party in Norway. It advocates for limited government intervention, lower taxes, and a conservative approach to social and cultural issues.
